Project Butterfly

International JDEdwards World rollout

Project Butterfly

A third-party service provider was employed to perform the majority of the development work for the Japanese, APAC and Benelux territories. I acted as a consultant for the Finance and Role Based Security modules within JD Edwards World, Robot Scheduler and the IBM Websphere MQ interfaces. Due to the size of the operation in Japan, I designed a new role-based security configuration which I was privileged to deliver in person in Japan. The Japanese office requested I return to Japan to provide the onsite go-live hyper care support.

Project Chrysalis

Business transformation project

Project Chrysalis

As IT Finance Stream Lead, I identified gaps which needed resolutions to enable the finance teams to perform their current tasks on the new vanilla JD Edwards. I designed and developed a new self-bill process which allowed line level matching of the self-bill invoices from the customer to the shipments from the warehouse, giving the Order to Cash team full visibility of outstanding despatches for the first time. A second role on the project was to work with the business to translate their job roles into role-based security configuration within JD Edwards.
